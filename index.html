Courses Offered By Us

We provide a intense learning program for all our students with best in teaching and learning curve for our students.

Nursery

These are pre-schools that tend to cater for children aged between 3 and 5, offering early education in the couple of years before they begin "big school".

LKG

LKG, Lower Kindergarten acts as preschool education. Children are taught in a playful manner such as playing, singing, drawing, etc.

UKG

The class for the student of UKG begins with social behavior. For example: Wishing teachers, sitting properly, and taking care of belongings with proper demonstrations.
